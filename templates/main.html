About Us

At Larkweave, we believe that specialized knowledge in trades, crafts, and cultural practices is a living legacy, meant to be shared and celebrated across generations. Founded to address the urgent crisis of disappearing expertise, our platform is a vibrant bridge connecting retiring professionals with the next wave of artisans, learners, and creators.

Our mission is to preserve and transfer the intricate techniques and time-honored traditions that define our world’s diverse crafts—from woodworking to weaving, pottery to storytelling. Using AI-assisted documentation, we capture the wisdom of master artisans through rich video, text, and 3D models, ensuring no detail is lost. Our interactive learning modules make these skills accessible, blending cutting-edge technology with hands-on practice.

Larkweave is a name that carries a vivid and meaningful connection to our Cross-Generation Knowledge Transfer Platform, blending the imagery of a lark—a small, spirited bird known for its joyful song—with the concept of weaving, a craft that symbolizes creation and connection.

How It Works

Larkweave connects artisans and learners to preserve crafts and trades. Here's how:

  • Capture Skills: Retiring experts document techniques via AI-assisted video, text, and 3D models.
  • Learn Easily: Interactive modules blend multimedia for hands-on craft mastery.
  • Find Mentors: Get matched with artisans for remote, scheduled mentorship.
  • Track Progress: Upload work samples for feedback and skill verification.
  • Join Forums: Share ideas and solve challenges in specialized craft communities.
  • Sell Creations: Showcase projects in our marketplace for handmade goods.

Larkweave Documentation

Empowering Cross-Generational Learning through AI and Mentorship

For Mentors

1. Signup

Sign up as a mentor on Larkweave to begin your knowledge-sharing journey.

2. Mentor Dashboard

Complete your profile by selecting your skillset, availability, and areas of expertise. This helps us match you with suitable learners.

3. Course Upload

To create a course:

  • Enter your email and paste the YouTube link of your video (ensure the video is public).
  • Our AI pipeline will:
    • Transcribe the video using OpenAI Whisper
    • Generate a summary and learning content using Google Gemma-3-27B-IT
  • Add multiple modules using the "+ Add Content" button. Each module requires a title and description.
  • Keep videos under 5 minutes to avoid long processing times.
  • Upload final product images to generate a 3D model via Dream Gaussian.

All data is stored securely in Firestore.

4. Forums

Set a username to participate in community forums. Ask questions, provide answers, or share techniques related to your craft.

5. Learner Progress

Track learner submissions through your dashboard. Download their uploaded files (images, documents), review their work, and leave constructive feedback or comments.

6. Marketplace

View a curated gallery showcasing outstanding completed projects by learners who have benefited from your mentorship and courses.

For Learners

1. Signup

Sign up as a learner to begin your learning journey with Larkweave. Explore crafts and find mentors.

2. Learner Dashboard

Complete your profile with your interests, current skill level, and learning availability. Based on this, we’ll suggest suitable mentors and courses. You'll be notified via email upon matching.

3. Course

Access your enrolled courses. Content includes mentor-provided videos, AI-generated summaries for quick reviews, detailed learning materials, and interactive modules or quizzes.

4. Forum

Set a username to engage in community forums. Ask questions about techniques, seek advice on challenges, help fellow learners, or share your project ideas and progress.

5. Progress Upload

As you work through a course, upload your project progress (photos, videos, descriptions). Your assigned mentor can view these uploads, provide comments, and offer guidance to support your learning.

6. Marketplace

Outstanding final submissions may be selected and featured in the public marketplace gallery, showcasing learner achievements and the quality of mentorship on Larkweave.
