Become a Mentor

Share your skills, preserve your knowledge, and impact future generations. Here's how you can upload your mentorship material to our platform.

Step 3: Upload Supporting Resources

Share your YouTube link and files with our team—we’ll handle the rest.

Our AI generates a brief, extracts learning objectives, and creates:

  • 📝 Auto-generated notes
  • 📊 Interactive quizzes
  • 🗂️ Learning modules

Your content is published on our learning dashboard for learners to watch, learn, and grow.
